# **App Name**: AgroSalso Direct

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Product Catalog: Browse and filter agricultural machinery by categories, brands, price range, and availability, with advanced sorting options.
- Detailed Product Pages: View comprehensive product information, high-resolution images, full specifications, and options to request a quote.
- Lead Generation Inquiry System: A dedicated modal form for visitors to submit offer requests or contact inquiries for specific products or general questions.
- Administrative Content Panel: A secure admin interface for AgroSalso staff to manage products, categories, brands, promotions, and review customer inquiries.
- AI-Powered SEO Meta Generator: Within the admin panel, a tool to automatically generate optimized meta titles and descriptions for product pages based on their specifications.
- Comprehensive SEO and Performance Suite: Features dynamic sitemap generation, structured data (JSON-LD), optimized images, and Server Components for superior search engine visibility and loading speed.

## Style Guidelines:

- A sophisticated light color scheme. The primary brand color is a deep industrial green ('#1A4D2E'), representing professionalism and the agricultural sector's robust nature. This is complemented by a vibrant, high-contrast accent yellow ('#F4A827') for calls-to-action, highlights, and promotions, creating an energetic feel without being overly rustic. The dominant background features a clean, barely off-white ('#F8F9F6') to ensure clarity and precision, aligning with a modern B2B aesthetic. Text colors are deep neutrals, providing excellent readability.
- Headings use 'Syne', a geometric sans-serif font, for a bold, modern, and authoritative presence. Body text is set in 'DM Sans', a clean and highly legible sans-serif, ensuring professional presentation across all content. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Icons combine minimalist line-art and subtle geometric forms to reinforce the 'industrial-precise' visual tone. Emoji icons are strategically used in specific UI elements like category strips to add a touch of approachability without compromising professionalism.
- A robust, grid-based layout emphasizing cleanliness, ample negative space, and precise alignment. Content is centered with a maximum width of 1440px and generous horizontal padding (56px) on desktop to provide a focused and high-end B2B appearance, akin to leading industrial equipment sites.
- Purposeful micro-animations and smooth transitions, primarily leveraging Framer Motion, enhance user experience. These include subtle fade-in and slide-up effects for elements on scroll, responsive hover effects for cards (e.g., subtle lift and shadow increase), animated underlines, and product card floating animations, all with carefully tuned durations and easing functions.
